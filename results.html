Message from the High School Principal and Athletics Office:

As we draw the curtain closed on what has been a fantastic IASAS Basketball tournament, our congratulations go out to all players, coaches, parents, officials and helpers who have all contributed so much to the success of this event. The games were played in true IASAS spirit, with teams humble in victory and gracious in defeat. Many new friendships were made and existing ties were strengthened. We wish all our seniors the very best as they turn their focus to life after High School and we look forward to seeing our up and coming stars returning to IASAS basketball in the years to come. Safe travels to one and all and thank you - it was a pleasure to host IASAS Basketball!

Steve Clark,
JIS High School Principal


Congratulations to all athletes for your successful participation in the 2014 IASAS Basketball Tournament. Your commitment to building positive spirit on the court and in the stands was exemplary all week. You did your schools and IASAS proud!

Nevin Odden,
JIS High School Athletics Coordinator
